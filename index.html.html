School Spotlight
Kibasila Primary — A Benchmark Transformation
Kibasila Primary School in Dar es Salaam became one of the first schools in Tanzania to fully transition from firewood to electric pressure cooking. With 5 EPCs installed, the school now prepares meals for 400 students in under 90 minutes — down from 4+ hours over open fires. Monthly fuel costs dropped from TZS 420,000 to under TZS 95,000, an 77% reduction. CO₂ emissions fell by 85%. Teachers report improved kitchen air quality and less time spent on firewood procurement, freeing staff for educational activities.
Impact Story
Nkuhungu School, Dodoma — Scale at Speed
Nkuhungu School deployed the largest fleet of any single school — 12 EPCs serving 1,100 students. Using a mix of 55L and 75L units, the school achieves maximum kitchen throughput. Real-time smart meters allow the school administration to track daily usage and report instantly to the district education office. This model is now being replicated across Dodoma region.
